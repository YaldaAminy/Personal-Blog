About Me

Hi! I'm Yalda Aminy, a passionate girl from Afghanistan with a heart full of dreams and determination. I’ve always been curious about how things work, especially in the world of technology. From exploring my mom’s phone as a child to learning more about the tech world, my journey has been full of courage and discovery. Despite the challenges, I’ve found strength in education and in standing up for girls who are often silenced.

I deeply love reading books, learning about technology, and creating art. My future goal is to build a platform that empowers marginalized girls by helping them tell their stories and turn them into inspiring products. I also dream of studying political science, owning a peaceful house filled with flowers, and writing books full of poetry and hope.

My Top 3 Role Models

  1. My Mother - Her resilience, strength, and unwavering support for me have shaped who I am today.
  2. Fereshteh Forough - Founder of the Code to inspire a coding school and a pioneer in teaching technology to Afghan girls. She inspires me to pursue my educational and professional goals in technology.
  3. Fallaci - As a bold and fearless journalist, her courage in addressing tough global issues and speaking truth to power is truly inspiring.

These role models have inspired me in many ways and continue to motivate me in my journey.
